FAQs

  • How is the speaking section scored?

    The speaking section is scored by AI technology that evaluates pronunciation, fluency, content, and other linguistic aspects.

  • Can I use fillers like "um" and "uh" during my response?

    While minimal use is acceptable, excessive fillers can affect your fluency score. Strive for clear, confident speech.

  • Do I need to speak in an accent other than my natural accent?

    No, you should speak naturally. The focus is on effective communication, not adopting a specific accent.

  • How is the writing module evaluated?

    AI technology assesses your written responses based on grammar, vocabulary, content, coherence, and organization.

  • Should I include personal opinions in the essay task?

    Personal opinions are acceptable, but ensure your response is well-balanced, supported, and coherent.

  • Can I use bullet points or numbered lists in the summary writing task?

    No, use complete sentences in the summary writing task. Bullet points aren't suitable for this module.

  • Is skimming and scanning recommended for all reading tasks?

    Is skimming and scanning recommended for all reading tasks?

  • About Reading Fill in the Blanks

    A passage with several gaps will appear on the screen & a list of options will be provided at the bottom of the passage. A student will have to drag a word from the below options & drop it at an appropriate blank. Student’s Reading skills will be assessed in this question type.

  • About Multiple Choice, Multiple Answers

    After reading a text, answer a multiple-choice question on the content or tone of the text by selecting more than one response. This question carries negative marking & hence, a student will get penalized if chosen a wrong answer.

  • How is the listening module structured?

    The listening module comprises various tasks, including multiple-choice, fill in the blanks, and summarize spoken text.

  • Can I adjust the volume during the listening tasks?

    You can adjust the volume using the controls provided. Make sure you're comfortable with the volume before the task begins.

  • How are accents and pronunciation evaluated in the listening section?

    Accents vary, and the focus is on understanding the content. Familiarise yourself with different accents through practice..
